The Decision

You're at the park alone, you went to the park because you wanted some alone time. But that silence was interupted by some screaming. You decide that you want go home, which pathway do you decide to take.

You've decided to take the brightest pathway, but it's where the screaming is coming from.

You've decided to take the other path, away from where the screaming is, but it's much darker.

You decide to further go into the path, and lead yourself to the exit, turns out that all that screaming was just props for a haunted house down the block, so there's nothing to worry about.

Continuing further to the path, something is off, there's no source of lights. While getting closer to the exit. You suddenly hear laughter. Only to notice a clown. You were never heard of again.
